Yamata Investments operates on the principles of stewardship, long-termism, and tactical patience.

### The Sword in the Tail
The legend of **Yamata no Orochi** teaches us that the greatest prizes are often hidden where others fear to look. Just as Susanoo found the legendary Kusanagi sword within the tail of the eight-headed serpent, we search for hidden value within complex market structures.

<div class="yamata-element">
    "Eight heads, eight tails, and eyes as red as winter cherries." — We remain vigilant across all facets of the market, but we only strike when the outcome is asymmetric.
</div>

### Our Pillars
- **Stewardship:** We manage capital with the multi-generational perspective, prioritizing preservation.
- **Patience:** We do not chase the noise of the many heads; we wait for the single, correct moment to act.
- **Precision:** Every allocation is a calculated strike, backed by exhaustive research and a significant discount to intrinsic value – our Margin of Safety.
- **Resilience:** Our strategy is built to endure the storms of the geopolitical landscape.
