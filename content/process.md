Our investment framework is a disciplined multi-stage journey from broad macro themes to precision entries.

### I. Thesis Formation
We identify global megatrends and structural shifts in fiscal policy to define our investment universe.

### II. Quality Screening
We filter for high-quality businesses with strong return profiles and healthy balance sheets, ensuring we avoid common value traps.

### III. Fundamental Audit
We conduct deep-dive analysis into operations and cash flow, benchmarking companies against their specific sector peers.

### IV. Valuation & Safety
We only initiate positions when the market price offers a significant margin of safety relative to our conservative fair-value estimates.

### V. Risk & Sizing
Capital is allocated based on conviction levels, with strict limits to ensure portfolio resilience.

### VI. Execution
We utilize patient entry protocols and maintain liquid reserves to capitalize on macro-driven volatility.

### VII. Continuous Monitoring
We regularly audit our theses and maintain the discipline to exit when our edge has been realized or the narrative shifts.
