Philosophy

The Yamata Philosophy

In Japanese mythology, the Yamata no Orochi is an eight-headed, eight-tailed serpent — a figure of overwhelming scale and complexity, coiled across eight valleys and eight peaks. It cannot be reasoned with directly. It can only be understood one head at a time, and defeated by patience rather than force.

Global markets present the same structure: eight-headed in their apparent complexity, indifferent to a single narrative, and hostile to anyone hoping to grasp them all at once. Yamata Investments exists to bring a disciplined, multi-dimensional framework to that chaos — not to tame the whole serpent, but to identify the single head, in the single moment, where the outcome is asymmetric.

Stability Through Order

We do not chase momentum. We master the underlying structures of value, so that even in the face of immense market noise, our approach remains focused, deliberate, and unhurried.

About

A private discipline, not a public pitch

Yamata Investments is a private investment vehicle focused on identifying and capturing structural inefficiencies across global markets. It operates at the intersection of fundamental rigour and tactical agility — while the broader market reacts to the noise of the day, Yamata remains focused on the signal beneath it.

The approach combines institutional-grade discipline with the freedom of independent, personally-held capital: no external mandate to satisfy, no quarter-end pressure to perform, and no obligation to be invested in anything the framework has not already earned its way into.

This is not a fund raising capital, and this site is not a solicitation. It exists as a record of a philosophy and a framework — for the discipline it imposes on its own author as much as for anyone reading it.

On Discretion

Position-level detail, current holdings, and performance figures are held privately. What is shared here is the architecture of the decision-making — not the decisions themselves.

Structure

Three quiet strategies, one framework

Capital is deployed across three independent but philosophically aligned strategies, each governed by the same seven-step discipline, each with its own risk envelope.

I. Equities

Systematic Equity Selection

A seven-gate committee process — macro, fundamental, valuation, reflexivity, sentiment, risk, and monitoring — applied to individual equity positions with a long holding horizon and a genuine margin-of-safety requirement at entry.

II. Derivatives

Risk-Defined Options Strategies

A volatility-regime-aware options book with defined maximum loss on every structure. Positions are sized against a modelled, not assumed, distribution of outcomes.

III. Digital Assets

Systematic Digital Asset Strategies

A signal-driven approach to digital asset markets, built to distinguish genuine independent signal from leveraged exposure to the asset class's dominant beta.

Values

The sword in the tail

The legend of Yamata no Orochi teaches that the greatest prize is often hidden exactly where others are too afraid to look. When the god Susanoo finally slew the serpent, he found the legendary blade Kusanagi coiled within its tail — the least accessible, most overlooked part of the beast. We search for the same hidden value within complex market structures.

I.

Stewardship

Capital is managed with a multi-generational perspective. Preservation is never subordinate to growth.

II.

Patience

We do not chase the noise of the many heads. We wait for the one correct moment, and act only then.

III.

Precision

Every allocation is a calculated strike — backed by exhaustive research and a genuine discount to intrinsic value, never a rounded-off estimate.

IV.

Resilience

The framework is built to endure the storms of the geopolitical landscape, not merely to perform when conditions are calm.
